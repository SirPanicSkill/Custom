    • OF APPLICATION

    - After washing your face, clean up the skin with toner.
    - Apply patches under your eye zone or on any part of face you want, using the spatula.
    - Press tightly to the skin, tapping with finger tips.
    - Remove the patches after 20-30 minutes and lightly tap the remaining essence into your skin.

    For intensive anti-puffiness effect store patches in the refrigerator.
