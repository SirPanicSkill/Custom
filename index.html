MODE OF APPLICATION

- After washing your face, clean up the skin with toner.
- Apply patches under your eye zone or on any part of face you want, using the spatula.
- Press tightly to the skin, tapping with finger tips.
- Remove the patches after 20-30 minutes and lightly tap the remaining essence into your skin.

For very dry skin or skin with deep wrinkles, hold the patches up to 40 minutes.
For intensive anti-puffiness effect store patches in the refrigerator.
- Use patches when needed for «Cinderella effect», improving your skin condition immeadiately, or daily during 30 days for stable long-term results.
- We would recommend to start using patches at the age of 25, when skin natural renewal processes begin to slow down.
- Peptides and Red Ginseng Patches provide the most powerful anti-age effect out of all four and are especially recommended for 45+ age.
- Patches can be applied not only under/around eyes, but on any other face spot.
- Still Hyaluronic Acid and Marine Complex patches with strong anti-puffiness effect may be used at an earlier age if needed.
- Collagen and Black Snail as well as Snail and 24K Gold are perfect to fight first signs of aging and preserving skin natural youthful appearance.
